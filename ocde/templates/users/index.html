About

A lot of people use Windows, which is not the best suited environment for programming (lately however they have been much more welcoming to LINUX). In general, it's terrible when you're required to work on a software that's not compatible with your operating system of choice. One thing that has marvelled at inter-OS compatibility is web browsers. In a not-so far future when high speed internet becomes as common as a smartphone or satellite TV, browsers will do most of the heavy lifting on computers. We'll be able to stream high end games and play on any system, (I'm actually having a hard time thinking about what browsers can't do already). One of these things that we already see in action is programming.

We, a team of 4 present you BACILISK, an online competing and development environment that allows you as a programmer to practice, code, compete on pretty much all popular/common programming languages right from your browser.
